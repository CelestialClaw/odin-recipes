Burrito

These bean, cheese, and rice burritos will keep you coming for more! Customize your burrito with whatever you want!

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Pinto Beans
  • Shredded Cheese, any
  • Cooked Rice, any
  • Optional add-ons:Cilantro, sour cream, guacamole, lime, etc.

Steps

  1. Warm up tortillas
  2. Place about 3 tablespoons of beans
  3. Place about 3 tablespoons rice
  4. Garnish with cheese
  5. Add optional add-ons, if doing so
  6. Wrap up tortilla!
  7. Optional:Grill burrito after wrapping by placing it in a hot pan and cooking on both sides
