Cheeseburgers

These cheeseburgers are out of your world! Take a bite into the juiciest patties with this foolproof recipe!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger meat
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Buns
  • Seasonings
  • Mayo, Ketchup, Mustard etc.

Steps

  1. Mix raw hamburger meat with seasonings and shit, whatever you like, bitch
  2. Form raw meat into patties
  3. Cook on stove until done
  4. Place cheese on top
  5. Grill buns until toasty
  6. Spread mayo on buns
  7. Place patty with cheese on bun then top with the rest of the ingredients
